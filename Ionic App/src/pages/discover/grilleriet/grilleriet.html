Grilleriet

Storgata 21, 0184 Oslo

Average visit: 30 minutes

When you enter the Folketeater Passage you will be greeted by an enchanting scent of smoldering charcoal. This is Grilleriet.
Inside our doors a welcoming room, a cozy bar in the middle of the restaurant and a large and open kitchen are waiting.
Much of Grilleriet's unique character is the food cooked on a charcoal grill for indoor use. The grill is the very heart of the restaurant and ensures that the food keeps its juicyness.
